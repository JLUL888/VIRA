FAQ

Common Questions

How much does popcorn ceiling removal cost?

Most homeowners pay between $1–$2 per square foot for standard popcorn ceiling removal. A 1,500 sq ft home typically runs $1,500–$3,000 depending on height, condition, and asbestos testing needs.

Do I need asbestos testing first?

Homes built before 1978 may have asbestos in popcorn ceilings. We always recommend testing first — it's inexpensive and our contractors can help arrange it.

How long does the work take?

A typical 3-bedroom home takes 1–2 days for ceiling removal and another 1–2 days for texture and touch-up. We'll give you a specific timeline in your quote.

Are your contractors licensed and insured?

Yes. We only work with licensed, insured contractors. We verify credentials before any referral is made.

Do you do the work yourselves?

Mr. Knockdown is a referral service. We match homeowners with the right local contractor for their project — ensuring quality, competitive pricing, and reliable work.

Is the quote really free?

Yes — completely free, no obligation. You're not committing to anything by requesting a quote. We just connect you with a contractor who will assess and price your project.
